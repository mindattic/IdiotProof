Market Score System

The heart of IdiotProof's decision-making: a composite indicator that combines 11 technical signals into a single actionable score from -100 to +100.

What is the Market Score?

The Market Score is THE single source of truth for trading decisions in IdiotProof. It takes multiple technical indicators, each providing different information about price action, and combines them into one unified score:

  • +100: Maximum bullish signal (all indicators aligned bullish)
  • +70 to +100: Strong bullish - consider entering long
  • +30 to +70: Moderate bullish - cautious optimism
  • -30 to +30: Neutral - no clear direction
  • -70 to -30: Moderate bearish - cautious pessimism
  • -100 to -70: Strong bearish - consider entering short
  • -100: Maximum bearish signal

How Each Indicator Contributes

Indicator Weight Bullish Signal Bearish Signal
MACD 18% MACD > Signal, histogram rising MACD < Signal, histogram falling
EMA Stack 15% Price > EMA(9) > EMA(21) > EMA(50) Price < all EMAs, inverted stack
ADX 15% ADX > 25 with +DI > -DI ADX > 25 with -DI > +DI
RSI 12% RSI 30-50 (oversold bounce) RSI 70+ (overbought)
VWAP 10% Price > VWAP Price < VWAP
Volume 8% High volume on up moves High volume on down moves
Stochastic 6% %K > %D, rising from oversold %K < %D, falling from overbought
Bollinger 5% Price bouncing off lower band Price rejected at upper band
OBV 5% OBV rising (accumulation) OBV falling (distribution)
CCI 3% CCI > 0, momentum positive CCI < 0, momentum negative
Williams %R 3% %R > -50, rising %R < -50, falling

Score Calculation

Each indicator produces a raw score from -100 to +100. These are then multiplied by their weights and summed: 

// Pseudocode for score calculation
TotalScore = (VwapScore × 0.10) +
             (EmaScore × 0.15) +
             (RsiScore × 0.12) +
             (MacdScore × 0.18) +
             (AdxScore × 0.15) +
             (VolumeScore × 0.08) +
             (BollingerScore × 0.05) +
             (StochasticScore × 0.06) +
             (ObvScore × 0.05) +
             (CciScore × 0.03) +
             (WilliamsRScore × 0.03)

Learned Weights

The default weights are just a starting point. IdiotProof learns optimal weights per ticker through genetic algorithms and gradient descent optimization:

💡 Weight Learning

Different stocks respond better to different indicators. TSLA might respond strongly to momentum (higher MACD weight), while SPY might be more VWAP-driven. The system learns these preferences automatically.

Learned weights are saved to Data/{TICKER}.weights.json and loaded automatically when trading that ticker.

LSTM Score Enhancement

The final score can be adjusted by the LSTM neural network predictor, which provides up to ±25 points based on its prediction: 

AdjustedScore = MarketScore + LstmAdjustment

// Where LstmAdjustment = Direction × Confidence × 25
// Direction: -1.0 (bearish) to +1.0 (bullish)
// Confidence: 0.0 to 1.0

// Example:
// LSTM predicts bullish (+0.7) with 80% confidence
// Adjustment = 0.7 × 0.8 × 25 = +14 points

This allows the AI to boost or dampen the score based on patterns it detects that aren't captured by traditional indicators.

Single Source of Truth

⚠️ Critical Architecture Decision

MarketScoreCalculator is the ONLY place where score calculation logic exists. Both live trading and backtesting use this exact same code. This ensures backtesting results accurately predict live trading performance.
